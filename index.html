About

I’m a graduate student focused on data engineering and analytics engineering. I enjoy designing systems that are repeatable, easy to validate, and easy for other people to use without having to “guess” what a metric means.

I usually start from the decision a dataset needs to support, then work backwards into ingestion, modeling, and quality checks. I care about clean contracts, sensible defaults, and pipelines that are safe to rerun.
