Nexora-NX

Nexora-NX converts unstructured text and PDFs into an explainable knowledge graph. It extracts entity–relationship triplets, builds a graph in-memory, and lets you explore connections with real reasoning paths.

LLM ExtractionNetworkXGraph AnalyticsStreamlitPyVis

What it does

  • Ingests raw text / PDFs and extracts entities + relationships.
  • Creates triplets and builds a knowledge graph (nodes + edges) with evidence metadata.
  • Supports centrality ranking (PageRank) and path-based reasoning (“How is X connected to Y?”).
  • Returns explainable answers based on the graph path + evidence.

Why it stands out

Most student projects stop at “LLM output”. This is a system: structured extraction → graph model → analytics → explainable responses. It’s the kind of architecture recruiters recognize as real engineering thinking.
