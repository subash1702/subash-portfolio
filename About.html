About

I’m Subash. I build data pipelines and analytics systems that stay reliable when the inputs are messy and the stakes are real. I care about clean interfaces, clear ownership, and making sure downstream teams don’t have to second-guess the data.

How I work

I start from the decision: who needs the data, what they’re trying to do, and what “good enough” means in practice. Then I work backwards to the source. I like making systems boring in the best way: predictable, testable, and easy to maintain.

I’m especially interested in streaming + batch patterns, data quality and contracts, and building “analytics-ready” datasets that scale.

What I’m looking for

Roles where I can build and own pipelines end-to-end, collaborate closely with product / analytics, and ship systems that people actually use.

  • Data Engineer
  • Analytics Engineer
  • AI Engineer (data + systems side)
  • Data Analyst / AI Analyst (when the work is deeply data-driven)
  • Business Analyst (when the work is product + operations heavy)
