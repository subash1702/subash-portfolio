About

I’m a Master’s graduate in Analytics at Northeastern. I enjoy building pipelines that are simple to trust: clean inputs, clear contracts, reliable transformations, and datasets that stay correct as data volume grows. I’m especially interested in streaming + batch systems where correctness matters as much as speed.

How I work
  • I start from the decision: who uses the data, what question it answers, and what “correct” means.
  • I design for late arrivals and retries early, so the system stays stable in production.
  • I prefer simple, testable transformations over clever logic that’s hard to maintain.
What I’m looking for
  • Data Engineering / Analytics Engineering roles
  • Work that involves pipelines, modeling, platform reliability, and data quality
  • Teams that care about clarity and ownership
