About

Hi, I’m Subash.

I’m a Master’s in Analytics student at Northeastern who enjoys building the kind of data foundations people stop worrying about. I’m usually happiest when I’m taking a noisy source, defining what “correct” means, and turning it into something teams can ship decisions on.

How I work
  • I start from the decision: who’s using the data and what changes if it’s wrong.
  • I like predictable pipelines: clear contracts, idempotency, and checks that fail loudly.
  • I keep things maintainable: small modules, readable SQL, and documentation that someone else can run.
What I’m building toward
  • Data engineering roles where reliability and scale matter.
  • Applied AI work that’s explainable, not magic — especially LLM + retrieval + structured systems.
  • Analytics engineering setups where downstream teams can move fast without breaking trust.
