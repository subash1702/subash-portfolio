About

I’m the kind of person who enjoys the part of data work that most people want to skip. The messy join logic, the weird edge cases, the late events, the “why is this number changing?” conversations. I like taking that uncertainty and turning it into something stable: a pipeline, a clean model, and outputs a team can rely on.

Right now I’m finishing my Master’s in Analytics at Northeastern, and I’m aiming for roles where I can build and own production-quality data systems: Data Engineer, Analytics Engineer, and AI / data roles that sit close to the platform.

How I work

  • I start with the decision and work backwards to the data.
  • I treat reliability as a feature, not a nice-to-have.
  • I prefer simple designs that scale over clever designs that break.
  • I write pipelines so someone else can maintain them without guessing.
