About

I’m a graduate student in analytics with a background in engineering, and I’m focused on building reliable data and AI systems. What I enjoy most is taking messy, real-world inputs and shaping them into something structured, trustworthy, and usable.

I tend to start from first principles: what does the data actually represent, what decision is it meant to support, and what would make the output dependable over time. I like work that sits between engineering and analysis — pipelines, analytical layers, and tools that help teams move faster without losing confidence in the numbers.
